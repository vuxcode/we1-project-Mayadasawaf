Yougert salad


Yougert salad amounts

  • A cup of yogurt
  • A spoonful of olive oil.
  • Salt, garlic, to taste.
  • A little water.
  • Dry or green mint.

    • How to prepare it
  • Chop the cucumber with the skin, then add the milk, water, salt and garlic,
    and mix all the ingredients together. Garnish the dish with olive oil and mint.
