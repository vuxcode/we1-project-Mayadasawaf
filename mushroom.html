Mushroom soup

Mushroom cream soup


Mushroom cream soup amounts

  • 50 gr (g) butter
  • One shallot, white part only, finely chopped
  • Mashed garlic clove
  • 500 Grams (gm) Mushrooms , chopped or thinly sliced
  • Chicken stock 2 cups
  • Puck bechamel sauce with cheese 500 milliliters (ml)
  • Thyme veins for decoration

    • How to prepare it
  • Melt the butter in a large bowl.
  • Add the shallots and garlic and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until soft.
  • Add the mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes, until tender.
  • Add the chicken stock and the bechamel sauce and bring to a boil, then leave the mixture on a low heat for two minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper. Pour soup into warm plates and garnish with thyme.
