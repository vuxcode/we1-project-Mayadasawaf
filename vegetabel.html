Vegetabel soup


  • Vegetabel soup amounts
  • A cup of green beans
  • a cup of peas.
  • One medium potato, cut into cubes.
  • One large carrot, cut into small cubes.
  • 4 pieces of zucchini, sliced.
  • 2 medium sized tomatoes, cut into medium cubes.
  • Spice spoon.
  • A spoonful of black pepper.
  • A teaspoon of cumin.
  • Chicken or meat soup, and it can be replaced with chicken broth..
  • Chopped medium onion.
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil .
  • a pinch of salt.
  • 2 minced garlic cloves.
  • Spice spoon.
  • A spoonful of black pepper.
  • A teaspoon of cumin.
  • Chicken or meat soup, and it can be replaced with chicken stock.
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil.

    • How to prepare it
  • Take a cooking pot and put the amount of olive oil in it, then add the chopped onion and stir until it changes color slightly,
  • Then add the garlic and stir it, but without browning it.
  • Add the potato cubes and carrots and saut them lightly in the onions and garlic.
  • Add green beans, peas, tomatoes and zucchini , then cook the previous mixture over medium heat for 7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add the spices, salt, pepper and cumin to the mixture, stir them slightly, then add the soup in an appropriate amount or the chicken broth cube and water.
  • Let the soup simmer for 15 minutes, until the ingredients have softened, and are ready to be served.
